Research

I'm now doing research under GEMS Lab Lab led by Professor Danai Koutra.

Topic: Hash-based Alignment of Multiple Graphs

The undergoing project is a graph alignment problem. We aim to utilize locality sensitive hashing method to efficiently and effectively find potential matching pairs when given multiple large graphs. We've tested our method using synthetic datasets and combine with state-of-the art techniques to see how much performance can we gain when introducing our method.
